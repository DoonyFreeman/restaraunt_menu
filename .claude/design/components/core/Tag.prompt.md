Pill label for menu attributes (vegetarian, spicy, popular) and small metadata.

```jsx
<Tag tone="veg" dot>Вегетарианское</Tag>
<Tag tone="spicy" dot>Острое</Tag>
<Tag tone="top">Популярное</Tag>
```

- `tone`: `veg` (jade) · `spicy` (chili) · `top`/`gold` · `neutral`
- `dot` adds a leading status dot. Tones are earthy by design — never neon.
